STICKY BUMPS SURF WAX

SNAG AND GRAB TECHNOLOGY: Sticky Bumps changed the game in 1992 with Snag and Grab technology. They implemented a new additive in their wax causes the it to stick to the board and build up small, sticky bumps as you’re waxing, setting them apart from other surf waxes completely.

ENVIRONMENTALLY FRIENDLY: Every part of the wax making process is ecofriendly, making no waste. Everything is reused and recycled. And when you’re finished, Sticky Bumps biodegrades through an emulsifying process, sending it right back to the environment from which it came.

SIGNATURE SCENT: Not only does Sticky Bumps make reliable wax for all your boards, but it smells great too. Their signature blueberry scent makes it one of the most recognizable and well-loved waxes on the market. You’ll surf all day just to get a whiff of the scent.

MADE FOR SURFERS BY SURFERS: Who knows what you need when you’re in the middle of a huge wave better than a fellow surfer. All Sticky Bumps products are tested, designed, and made by other surfers, ensuring that each product is created with passion and experience.

PRODUCT SPECIFICATIONS: Package contains Sticky Bumps Original Surf Board Wax. Fresh from the factory. 80 grams per each bar. Original formula. For use above 75 degrees Fahrenheit.
